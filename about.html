Why SoberHouseManager?

We built this software because we needed it. As sober living home owners, we couldn't find a platform that handled everything simply, affordably, and reliably. So we created it — for ourselves and others like us.

SoberHouseManager simplifies rent collection, resident applications, and operations — so you can focus on supporting recovery, not chasing paperwork.
