Welcome to SoberHouseManager

SoberHouseManager is a platform created by sober living owners for sober living owners. We understand the need for simplicity, affordability, and transparency when it comes to managing recovery homes.

Our software is designed to streamline rent collection, resident applications, and backend operations — all in one clean, professional, and easy-to-use system. No more guesswork, no more spreadsheets.
