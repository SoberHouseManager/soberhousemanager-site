Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is this really free for owners?
    Yes. Owners and house managers pay nothing. A small fee is added to each resident transaction.
  • Can residents pay automatically?
    Yes. They can use cards or ACH with automatic debits on due dates.
  • Can I manage multiple homes?
    Absolutely. Each home can be set up separately under one login.
  • How secure is this?
    We use encrypted, compliant processors like Stripe.
